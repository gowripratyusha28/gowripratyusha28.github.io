Who are we?

We are a bunch (a little larger one, maybe) of KGPians from different backgrounds and with diverse passion who have come together to solve modern problems requiring fast-moving people and "KISS" solutions; our aim is to make the pool of innovations and research at IITs accessible and understandable to everyone; from students to scientists... and EVERYONE between.
